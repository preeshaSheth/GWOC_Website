Our Story

Every Coffee bean Tells a Story

The inception of Kôė was to create a micro scale coffee roasting and food curation experience, with a serene ambience to make the whole experience a complete package for the clientele. Creating a coffee roasting cafe involved a passion for coffee, a dedication to quality roasting, and a desire to create a welcoming space for coffee enthusiasts. The journey likely included learning about various coffee beans, perfecting roasting techniques, and curating a menu that showcases the rich flavors of different coffee origins. The cafe also emphasizes the importance of sustainability and fair trade practices in sourcing its beans, and promoting Indian coffee farmers.
